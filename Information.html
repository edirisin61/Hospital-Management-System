    We believe that the quality of care is paramount to both our patients and surgeons and we pride ourselves on our outstanding clinical reputation in the region.We have the best surgeons available in the region and can assure you in that you will be referring your patients to highly qualified and experienced medical practitioners.

    Our patient satisfaction surveys undertaken everyday at the hospital continue to provide us with outstanding feedback. Niroga Hospital is a proud member of the Sri Lanka Private Hospital Association and utilises the association's considerable influence of the private health sector.

    The Specialist Centre includes six consulting rooms and a procedure room where surgeons can consult with their patients and perform procedures under local Anaesthetic. Patients are able to consult with Specialists, have x-rays, MRI's and ultrasounds and also have their surgery performed in one convenient location.
